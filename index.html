About Us

Welcome to Kevlar Technologies, your one-stop shop for anything IT. We are committed to providing you with the finest IT support and solutions possible, with a focus on web and mobile development, hardware maintenance and repair, and graphic design. Kevlar Technologies, founded in 2018 by Jahbless Kofi Amedzekor, Tetteh-Bio Kirk Tettey, and Antenor Osei-Nyarko Nana, has gone a long way since its humble beginnings in Adenta. When we originally started out, our desire to be the one-stop shop for all things IT inspired them to launch their own company. We hope you love our items as much as we do making them available to you. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any questions or comments.
